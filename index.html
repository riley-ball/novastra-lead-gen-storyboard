Smart Automations (Optional Add-Ons)

Optional upgrades you can add anytime

Start simple, then upgrade: These are optional AI-powered automations you can add later as your needs grow. The core system works great on its own – these just make it even better.

When leads come in hot, you want to know immediately – not hours later buried in email.

Lead Handling

Hot Lead Alerts

SMS / WhatsApp

Get an instant text or WhatsApp message the moment a support coordinator clicks your email, replies, or fills out a form. Call them back while they're still thinking about you – not hours later.

Stuck Lead Nudges

Automated reminders

If a lead hasn't moved in your pipeline for 3+ days, get an automatic reminder to follow up. No more leads slipping through the cracks because life got busy.

Calls & Voicemail → Summary

AI transcription

Missed a call? AI transcribes the voicemail, summarizes the key points, and logs it against the right contact in your CRM. Also works for calls you take – get automatic notes without typing.

Fill vacancies faster with less manual work – the right people hear about openings automatically.

Vacancies & Referrals

Vacancy → Auto Campaign

Trigger-based outreach

When you mark a new vacancy in your system, automatically send a targeted email to support coordinators in that region who've shown interest before. Fill beds faster without lifting a finger.

Referral Intake + AI Summary

Smart form processing

When a referral comes in (email, form, or PDF), AI extracts the key details – participant needs, funding, location, support requirements – and creates a neat summary card in your CRM. No more re-reading long forms.

Simple Referral Portal

Branded web form

Give support coordinators a clean, branded webpage where they can submit referrals directly. No more email attachments or phone tag – everything lands in your CRM ready to action.

Let AI handle the repetitive work so you can focus on people, not paperwork.

Reporting & AI Helpers

Monthly Board-Ready PDF

Auto-generated reports

On the 1st of each month, automatically receive a polished PDF report showing: new leads, conversions, revenue by channel, and pipeline health. Ready to share with your board or investors – no spreadsheet wrangling.

AI Content Helper

Draft copy in seconds

Need to write a vacancy post, participant success story, or newsletter blurb? Give AI a few bullet points and get a polished draft in seconds. Edit and publish – instead of staring at a blank page.

Check-In & Feedback Prompts

Automated surveys

Automatically send a quick satisfaction check-in to participants or families at key milestones (30 days, 90 days). Collect feedback, spot issues early, and build a library of testimonials – all on autopilot.
